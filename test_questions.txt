# MLX-GRPO Evaluation Questions
# Test your fine-tuned model with these math word problems

## ⭐ EASY (Basic arithmetic)

1. Sarah has 12 apples and buys 8 more. How many apples does she have in total?
   Answer: 20

2. A pizza costs $15 and a drink costs $3. What is the total cost?
   Answer: 18

3. John ran 5 miles on Monday and 7 miles on Tuesday. How many miles did he run in total?
   Answer: 12

## ⭐⭐ MEDIUM (Multi-step reasoning)

4. A store has 48 shirts. They sell half of them on Monday and 12 more on Tuesday. How many shirts are left?
   Answer: 12

5. Emily earns $15 per hour and works 6 hours a day. If she works 5 days, how much does she earn in total?
   Answer: 450

6. A box contains 120 candies. If 3 friends share them equally, how many candies does each friend get?
   Answer: 40

## ⭐⭐⭐ HARD (Complex multi-step)

7. A farmer has 30 chickens. Each chicken lays 2 eggs per day. If the farmer sells eggs for $3 per dozen, how much money does he make in one day?
   Answer: 15

8. Maria saves $50 per month. After 8 months, she spends $120 on a gift. How much money does she have left?
   Answer: 280

9. A train travels 60 miles per hour. If it travels for 3.5 hours, how many miles does it cover?
   Answer: 210

## ⭐⭐⭐⭐ VERY HARD (GSM8K-style)

10. A baker makes 144 cookies and puts them in boxes of 12. He sells each box for $8. If he keeps 3 boxes for himself, how much money does he make?
    Answer: 72

11. Tom has $500. He spends 40% on rent, $80 on food, and saves the rest. How much does he save?
    Answer: 220

12. A school has 450 students. 2/5 are boys. Of the boys, 60% play sports. How many boys play sports?
    Answer: 108
